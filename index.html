gog

Personal Google briefing CLI for Peter Kim.

gog is a private command-line tool that generates a daily briefing for Peter Kim by reading Gmail and Google Calendar data directly from his own Google account.

What it does

Each morning, gog queries Gmail for relevant email activity and reads Google Calendar for the day's schedule. It synthesizes this into a private daily briefing — summarizing priorities, flagging important messages, and surfacing calendar context. Nothing is sent anywhere. It runs locally and exits.

Who uses it

One person: Peter Kim. gog is not a product, not distributed, not available for download. It exists solely to help one person stay on top of his day.

Scopes used

  • gmail.readonly — reads email to extract briefing content
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/calendar — reads and writes calendar for briefing context
